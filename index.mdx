## What Can you do with Veritus Ai?

With Veritus, you can search, read, and improve research all in one place. It helps you:

- Find papers fast – discover the top 100 most relevant studies in minutes.

- Understand complex research – ask questions and get source-linked answers with zero hallucinations.

- Strengthen manuscripts – analyze, compare, and refine drafts before submission.

- Run literature reviews – map key themes, gaps, and suggested papers in under an hour.

- Guide your team – share structured reports and feedback with students or co-authors.

## What makes Veritus Ai different?


- End-to-end workflows – Covers the full research cycle: paper discovery, deep reading, literature review, and manuscript improvement in one place.

- Source-verified answers – Every summary or insight links back to the original PDF, keeping work audit-ready and hallucination-free.

- Research-focused design – Built for professors, postdocs, and labs, not general users, with tools tailored for academic rigor.

- Cross-discipline coverage – Accesses over 200 million papers across STEM, social sciences, and humanities for comprehensive search.

- Collaboration-ready – Share structured reports and analysis with PhD students, co-authors, or lab members through secure links.

- Customizable workflows – Refine results by keywords, research goals, or uploaded reference papers to match your exact needs.

- Proven time savings – Professors at Kyoto University and IIT Roorkee save 490+ hours annually by using Veritus.ai instead of manual reviews.
